What we do

We fund self-help projects worldwide, focusing on small-scale initiatives that deliver immediate, practical benefits. Each project originates from the people directly involved, ensuring funds reach the right recipients while keeping administration costs minimal.

How it works

Projects are proposed by the people concerned through trusted contacts.
Members take part in deciding which projects receive support.
Funds are allocated with no administrative overhead beyond banking.

Funding

The primary source of funding is member contributions, complemented by donations and fundraising activities.
